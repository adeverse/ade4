# Cubic Ecological Data

`bf88` is a list of 6 data frames corresponding to 6 stages of
vegetation.  
Each data frame gives some bird species informations for 4 counties.

## Usage

``` r
data(bf88)
```

## Format

A list of six data frames with 79 rows (bird species) and 4 columns
(counties).  
The 6 arrays (S1 to S6) are the 6 stages of vegetation.  
The attribut 'nomesp' of this list is a vector of species French names.

## Source

Blondel, J. and Farre, H. (1988) The convergent trajectories of bird
communities along ecological successions in european forests.
*Oecologia* (Berlin), **75**, 83â€“93.
